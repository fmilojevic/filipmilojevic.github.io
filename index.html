About

I am a Research Specialist at the Normal Lab, working with Professor Leonardo Bursztyn. I was previously at LSE, where I obtained a Master's in Econometrics and Mathematical Economics (EME) and LMU Munich from where I hold a BSc in Economics. My work intersects behavioral IO, development economics, and political economy.
